Our Story

Why Legzeri

Legzeri began with a simple frustration: too many wardrobes are full of clothes that look good for one season and fall apart the next. We wanted to build the opposite — a small set of pieces that feel as good in year three as they did on day one.

Every fabric is chosen first for how it wears over time. Every cut is tested against the way people actually move through their day — sitting, walking, reaching, packing a bag. Nothing is added that doesn't earn its place.

We work in small batches with a handful of mills and workshops we visit in person, because quality is easier to protect when the supply chain is short enough to see.

— The Legzeri Studio
